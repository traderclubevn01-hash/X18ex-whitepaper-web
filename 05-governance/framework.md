# Governance Framework

## X18 DAO вҖ” Advanced Decentralized Governance

### Principles
X18 DAO adheres to the **Progressive Decentralization** model вҖ” starting from team-led to ensure development speed, gradually moving towards full on-chain governance as the protocol matures.

## Voting Power

| Mechanism | Voting Power |
|:---|:---|
| **$X18 (liquid)** | 1 X18 = 1 Vote |
| **veX18 (locked 6 months)** | 1 X18 = 1.5 Votes |
| **veX18 (locked 12 months)** | 1 X18 = 2 Votes |
| **veX18 (locked 24 months)** | 1 X18 = 3 Votes |
| **veX18 (locked 48 months)** | 1 X18 = 4 Votes |

**Delegation**: Holders can delegate voting power to a trusted representative.

## Progressive Decentralization Timeline

| Phase | Timeline | Governance Model |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Phase 0** | Pre-launch | Team-led, community advisory |
| **Phase 1** | Month 0-12 | Team-led + community input + multi-sig (3/5) |
| **Phase 2** | Month 12-24 | Council governance + multi-sig (5/9) |
| **Phase 3** | Month 24-36 | Full on-chain voting, council advisory |
| **Phase 4** | Month 36+ | Fully autonomous DAO |
